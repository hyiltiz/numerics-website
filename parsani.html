Research Interests

  • Numerical Analysis
    • Spatially high order discretizations for hyperbolic PDEs on unstructured grids
    • Efficient time integration/solution iterative approaches for spatially high order discretizations
    • Optimally efficient integrators
  • Turbulent flows
    • LES of internal flows
    • Aero-acoustics and noise propagation
  • Scientific computing
    • CFD solvers
    • Grid generation for CFD applications
    • Scientific software engineering
  • ...and recently inspired by Prof. Ketcheson:
    • Shallow water waves
