Research Interests

  • Numerical Analysis
    • Monotonicity and positivity of numerical methods
    • Runge-Kutta and General linear methods for initial value problems
    • High order schemes for hyperoblic PDEs
    • Low-storage algorithms
    • Optimally efficient integrators
  • Nonlinear waves in heterogeneous media
    • Elastic, electromagnetic, and shallow water waves
    • Periodic and random media
    • Solitary waves
    • Shock waves in heterogeneous media
  • Scientific computing
    • High performance parallel solution of hyperbolic PDEs
    • Scientific software engineering
